## Research Experience

### ParroTao: Personalized Training Analytics and Program Generation
**Project Lead** (Supervisor: Prof. Peng Yang, SUSTech), 09/2024 - Present

- Built an AI platform for personalized sports analytics using heterogeneous wearable data.
- Designed robust representation learning for heart-rate modeling under cross-device and cross-user heterogeneity.
- Developed a self-evolving multi-agent LLM pipeline for personalized training plan generation.
- Co-first-authored manuscript on robust heart-rate modeling; filed related patent applications.

### Multi-modal Topic Modeling for Single-cell Perturb-seq Data
**Research Assistant** (Supervisor: Prof. Yingxin Lin, CUHK), 09/2025 - Present

- Developing multimodal representation learning for large-scale Perturb-seq data.
- Integrating transcriptomics, perturbation identities, gene-trait supervision, and pretrained embeddings.
- Evaluating latent programs with prediction, enrichment, and interpretability metrics.

### Robust Multimodal Learning with Missing Modalities
**Research Assistant** (Supervisor: Prof. Doudou Zhou, NUS), 01/2025 - 09/2025

- Proposed pattern-aware shared/specific multimodal learning under missing modalities.
- Improved robustness via contrastive pretraining with random modality dropout.
- Validated on MIMIC-IV and achieved strong performance under diverse missing-modality settings.

### UWB/IMU-Fused Motion Capture for 3D CoM Estimation
**Team Member** (Supervisor: Prof. Mingming Zhang, SUSTech), 10/2022 - 08/2023

- Built a wearable sensing system for real-time 3D center-of-mass estimation.
- Integrated multi-IMU sensor fusion and UWB-based localization algorithms.
- Developed hardware-software prototypes for elderly-care related clinical validation.
